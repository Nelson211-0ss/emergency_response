Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our emergency response platform

What is UPF-emSERVICE?

UPF-emSERVICE is a real-time emergency reporting platform that connects citizens directly with law enforcement and emergency responders. It allows for quick reporting of incidents with multimedia evidence and location tracking capabilities.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Emergency reports are processed immediately and dispatched to the nearest available responders. Response times vary based on the severity of the emergency and available resources, but our system ensures the fastest possible coordination.

What types of emergencies can I report?

You can report various types of emergencies including medical emergencies, crimes in progress, fire incidents, traffic accidents, and suspicious activities. Our platform is designed to handle all types of emergency situations.

Can I attach photos or videos to my report?

Yes, our platform supports multimedia attachments including photos, videos, and audio recordings. These can be crucial pieces of evidence that help emergency responders better understand and respond to the situation.

What devices are compatible with the service?

Our service is compatible with all modern smartphones, tablets, and computers. We support the latest versions of iOS and Android for mobile devices, and all major web browsers for desktop access.

Do I need to enable location services?

While not mandatory, enabling location services is highly recommended as it helps emergency responders locate you quickly and accurately. You can enable or disable this feature at any time through your device settings.

How is my personal information protected?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. All data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized emergency response personnel when necessary.

Who has access to my emergency reports?

Your emergency reports are only accessible to authorized emergency response personnel and law enforcement officials directly involved in handling your case. All access is logged and monitored for security purposes.
